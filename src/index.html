Tozzi's on 12th

Tozzi's is a family-owned restaurant renowned for its Italian cuisine, seafood, and steaks. With over 50 years of culinary experience, Chef Michael Tozzi and his team prepare dishes using traditional Italian family recipes.

The Tozzi Grandparents sailed from Italy to America while still in their teens. The year was 1914 and they settled in Magnolia, Ohio. It was there that the family business was established. Now, as we establish our restaurant here in Canton, Ohio, with our Fourth Generation Tozzi children we welcome you to dine with us.
